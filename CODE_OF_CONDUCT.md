# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and constructive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Keep criticism focused on ideas, code, docs, and project outcomes.
- Give and receive feedback with patience.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, reviews, or discussions after being asked to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.

To report a conduct concern, contact a maintainer through the least-public channel available. If only public issues are enabled, ask for a private maintainer contact without including sensitive details.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project-managed communication channels.
